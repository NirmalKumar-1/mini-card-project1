Ranakpur Jain Temple

- Desuri, Rajasthan
Ranakpur Jain Temple, built in the 15th century, is famed for its 1,444 uniquely carved marble pillars. Dedicated to Tirthankara Adinatha, it showcases stunning symmetry, intricate design, and remains a timeless marvel of Jain architecture.
